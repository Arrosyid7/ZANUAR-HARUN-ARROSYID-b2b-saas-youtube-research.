# LinkedIn Insights - TK Kader (SaaS Growth Founder)

## Post Date: May 2026
**Topic:** Why 90% of B2B SaaS YouTube Channels Have Zero ROI

Most B2B SaaS companies treat YouTube like a graveyard for 45-minute webinar replays. Nobody wants to watch your internal Zoom recordings.

If you want your YouTube channel to drive pipeline and Free Trials, you need to shift from "Company News" to "Problem-Centric Content."

Here is the 3-part framework we use for SaaS clients:
1. **The Attract Video (Top of Funnel):** Answer the exact question your buyer types into Google when they are frustrated. (Example: "How to reduce customer churn in enterprise software").
2. **The Nurture Video (Middle of Funnel):** Show a case study of how a real company solved that problem. This is where your SaaS product is subtly introduced as the tool.
3. **The Convert Video (Bottom of Funnel):** Product walkthroughs and feature comparisons. 

Stop measuring views. Start measuring Assisted Conversions in your analytics.
